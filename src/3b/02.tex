$S, T \in \L(V,V)$. Since $\text{range\;}S \subset \text{null\;}T$, we have $(TS)(v) = 0$ for all $v \in V$.

Hence,
\begin{align*}
    (ST)^2(v) &= (STST)(v)                      \\
              &= S \big( (TS)(Tv) \big)         \\
              &= S (0)                          \\
              &= 0
\end{align*}
with the last equality holding because all linear maps take 0 to 0.
