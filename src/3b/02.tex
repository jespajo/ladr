\question{
    3.B.2.
    Suppose $S, T \in \L(V,V)$ are such that $\op{range}S \subseteq \op{null}T$.
    Prove that $(ST)^2 = 0$.
}

    Since $\op{range}S \subseteq \op{null}T$, we have $(TS)(v) = 0$ for all $v \in V$.

    Hence,
\begin{align*}
    (ST)^2(v) &= (STST)(v)                      \\
              &= S \big( (TS)(Tv) \big)         \\
              &= S (0)                          \\
              &= 0
\end{align*}
    with the last equality holding because all linear maps take 0 to 0.
